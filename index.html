About Hallie ⌄

Hallie is a creator, podcaster, and the sister people didn't always have. Host of My Sister's Closet — a podcast about emotional growth, real life, and the conversations women actually need to have.

She's also the creator of the My Inner Mind wellness app, My Inner Grove, and Innershelfstudio — a digital shop full of tools for your inner world. Everything lives at halliewho.com.

She's not here to give you advice. She's here to sit with you in it.
